About

01 / 05

I'm a Research Scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi, where my work sits at the intersection of vision, language, and control — building models that let autonomous systems understand a scene the way a person would describe it, and act on that understanding. Before this, I taught as an Assistant Professor at Lovely Professional University and spent a year as a KIAC Intern at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. My path here ran through an MTech in Artificial Intelligence at NIT Jalandhar and a BTech in Computer Software Engineering at Sri Sai University, Palampur.
